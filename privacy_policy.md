Last Updated: 2026-02-15

Your privacy is important. This document explains what data **Kelly** collects and how it is used.

## 1. Data Collected
Kelly may collect and store the following data **only as required for functionality**:

- Discord User IDs

- Moderation-related data (mutes, warns, bans, logs)

Kelly **does NOT** read or store private messages unless explicitly required by a command (e.g., moderation DMs).
